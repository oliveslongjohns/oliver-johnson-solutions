How do I use Web Scraping?

Regulatory change in UK law is an ever continuous process. I build scripts to pull data from online legal resources (e.g. the FCA's Handbook, legislation.gov.uk), run comparisons, analyse the latest changes, and highlight where our clients may be at risk.

How do I use LLMs?

Language Models are an incredibly powerful tool that can be used for far more than building chatbots.With the correct prompt engineering, an LLM can highlight where text contains obligations that are relevant to a business, and even draft legal guidance in plain English.

How do I use Time Series?

In Utilities, the most important data piece is energy consumption data. With a growing environmental crisis, and volatile energy markets, clients are prioritsing reducing their energy costs and CO2 emissions more than ever. You do this by analysing their consumption over time, and identifying where to reduce it.
